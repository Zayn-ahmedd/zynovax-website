Refund & Cancellation

Last Updated: June 7, 2026

1. Scope and Applicability

This Refund & Cancellation Policy outlines the billing, cancellation, and refund procedures governing the professional branding, strategic visual identity, design engineering, performance marketing, and copywriting services provided by Zynovax.

By signing our Service Contract, onboarding project details, or submitting retainer payments, you signify your agreement to these terms.

2. Project Advance Onboarding Payment

We pride ourselves on delivery of strategy-first, template-free custom work. Building premium assets requires allocation of strategic resources, research engineering, and operational capacity immediately upon sign-off.

Consequently, **once a service contract is signed, project onboarding begins, and project work commences, the initial advance onboarding payment is strictly non-refundable**. This policy compensates for the creative hours, competitor research, and resources reserved exclusively for your brand.

3. Monthly Recurring Retainers

For ongoing digital presence, copywriting, social media management, or performance marketing optimization contracts that operate on a recurring monthly retainer fee basis:

  • Cancellation Window: You may cancel your monthly retainer contract at any time.
  • Prior Written Notice: To ensure a clean off-boarding and avoid automated invoice generation, cancellations must be requested **at least 15 days prior to the next scheduled monthly billing date**.
  • Deliverables During Notice: Work, asset scheduling, and ad optimization will continue as planned during this 15-day notice window, ensuring no disruption to your brand's digital presence.

4. Exceptions and Modifications

If a project is cancelled by Zynovax due to internal constraints before any project strategy deliverables or onboarding exercises have commenced, a full refund of the received advance payment will be processed within 5 to 7 business days to the client's bank account.

5. How to Initiate Cancellation

All contract cancellation requests must be submitted in writing via email to info@zynovax.in. Our finance team will confirm receipt of the cancellation notice and confirm the off-boarding date within 24 hours.
