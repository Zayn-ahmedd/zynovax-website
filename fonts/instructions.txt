Copy your licensed CoFo Sans font files into this folder (d:/Zynovax/Website/fonts/).

To match the @font-face rules defined in style.css, please rename your files to match the following names:

1. For Regular Weight (400):
   - CoFoSans-Regular.woff2  (or CoFoSans-Regular.woff)

2. For Medium Weight (500):
   - CoFoSans-Medium.woff2   (or CoFoSans-Medium.woff)

3. For Bold Weight (700):
   - CoFoSans-Bold.woff2     (or CoFoSans-Bold.woff)

4. For Black Weight (800):
   - CoFoSans-Black.woff2    (or CoFoSans-Black.woff)

Once these files are placed in this folder, the website will automatically load and render CoFo Sans on all devices!
