Common Questions

Everything you need
to know

How long does the whole process take?

It depends on the scope of work. A brand strategy alone takes 7–14 days. A full brand build (strategy + identity + social setup) typically takes 3–6 weeks. Ongoing retainers begin delivering results within the first 30 days.

What happens if I'm not happy with the work?

Every project includes 2–3 rounds of structured revisions. We present work with context and ask for specific feedback — so revision rounds are focused and efficient. We don't stop until you're genuinely happy.

Do I need to have a brand already to work with you?

Not at all. We work with startups from scratch, established businesses looking to rebrand, and everything in between. Our process is designed to meet you wherever you are.

How involved do I need to be during the process?

We've designed the process to be efficient for busy founders. You'll need 2–3 hours for the discovery call, and then focused review sessions at key milestones. The rest is handled by us — so you can run your business.

Do you work with businesses outside Chennai?

Absolutely. We're based in Perungalathur, Chennai but work with clients across India and internationally. Our entire process is built to work remotely — calls, collaboration, and delivery all happen online, seamlessly.
