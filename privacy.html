Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 7, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Zynovax ("we", "our", "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your personal data when you visit our website (zynovax.in) and fill out our contact or audit request forms.

This policy is structured in compliance with India's **Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023**, and conforms to the advertising standards and guidelines set by Facebook (Meta) Ads and Google Ads.

2. Personal Data We Collect

We only collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when expressing interest in obtaining information about us or our services. This includes data collected through our contact forms, custom audit forms, and scheduler links:

  • Contact Information: Full Name, Email Address, and Phone Number.
  • Business Information: Business or Brand Name, Website URL, and Social Media (e.g., Instagram) profile links.
  • Project Details: Information about your business goals, target competitors, creative copy assets, and any additional details you submit in the audit scope text area.

3. How We Use Your Data

We process your personal data based on your explicit consent and for legitimate business interests. Specifically, we use your data to:

  • Conduct and deliver your custom **Free Brand Audit** report.
  • Initiate business communication, client onboarding, and project proposal discussions.
  • Address service requests and provide ongoing client customer support.
  • Send occasional transactional updates or proposals related to our brand engineering services.

4. Data Sharing and Protection

Your trust is paramount. **We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal data with third parties** for commercial or marketing purposes.

We apply robust security measures (encryption, restricted access control protocols) to protect your data from unauthorized access, modification, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Cookies and Tracker Pixel Compliance

We utilize cookies and tracking technologies (such as the Meta Pixel and Google Tag Manager) on our website to measure campaign performance and deliver optimized advertisements. These platforms process anonymized usage analytics according to their respective privacy standards. You can manage cookie consents or opt-out of behavioral targeting through your browser settings.

6. Your Rights Under the DPDP Act

Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, you possess clear rights as a Data Principal:

  • Right to Access: You can request a summary of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Correction & Erasure: You can ask us to correct, update, or permanently delete your personal records.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You can withdraw your consent for us to process your data at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please email us directly at info@zynovax.in. We will process and address your request within 48 hours.
